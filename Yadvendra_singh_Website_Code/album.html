Mortal Luminous wrote down LOST PIECES OF MINE to recall the unbreakable bond
between what a person have with his/her loved one which eventually fade away and left
behind without one knowing about it .

The Bands Says “Amount of emotion that you could feel in the lyrics is crazy.
As We listen to the song more and more it remind us of so many things ,
We've been through in my life.Valueable relationships, letting go of things I didn't want to
and just situations where happiness came with a price. We want our music to embrace
the pain that we feel losing someone close to us and the cycle of life.
